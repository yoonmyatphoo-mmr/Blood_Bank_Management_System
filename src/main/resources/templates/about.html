Can you be a blood donor?

  1. You must be between (18) and (55) years of age.
  2. For men, weight must be above 130 pounds.
  3. For women, weight must be above 130 pounds.
  4. He must be a good sleeper.
  5. Must not drink alcohol.
  6. Must be free of recent illness.
  7. In the past 6 months, injection pierced ears, He must not have had acupuncture.
  8. pregnancy breastfeeding She must not be menstruating recently.

Even if the above information is consistent, the doctors will take responsibility to ensure that your health is not harmed by donating blood.
